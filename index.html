Our Story

Where Every Sip
Tells a Story

Mamita's Cafe was born from a simple dream — to create a warm, cozy corner in San Narciso where every guest feels like family. Nestled in the heart of Zambales, we serve handcrafted drinks and delicious bites made with love and the finest ingredients.

From our signature matcha lattes to hearty snacks and refreshing frappes, every item on our menu is crafted to bring comfort and joy. Come as a stranger, leave as family. That is the Mamita's promise.
